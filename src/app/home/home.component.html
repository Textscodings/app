Land Contamination

Sources of Land Contamination in Construction Sites:

  • Improper maintenance of equipment.
  • Improper Storage of chemical substances and fuels.
  • Spillage of fuel during transfer and leaks.
  • Improper waste management.
  • Vehicle / Equipment washing and improper management.
  • Improper management of Sewage/ wastewater generated at the site.
  • Temporary and permanent physical or chemical changes to soil
    characteristics during stockpiling, leveling and earthworks.
  • Accidental spill or leakage of chemicals.
