Mitigation Measures

  • All hazardous liquids should be stores in an impervious bund area where
    the volume of the storage bund is not less than 110% of the largest storage
    tank contained within the bund.
  • All chemicals to be stored in a safe manner so as not to get damaged and
    cause spillages onto the floor.
  • Appropriate spill clean-up material must be present adjacent to storage
    and maintenance areas.
  • Used waste fuel or other waste chemicals shall be stored in a bunded area
    untill collected for off-site disposal by an approved Waste Contractor at
    CWM-AD approved site.
  • Training of workforce to clean up small and medium spills immediately and
    segregate the generated soil as hazardous waste.
  • A designated area for trucks is preferred where water is collected in a sump
    or pit and pumped into sealable containers and removed nu a license contractor.
